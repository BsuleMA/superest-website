the order process

download, register and explore options on our app

Begin your journey by downloading the Superest Delivery app from your device's app store for iOS devices or on play store for android devices. It's your gateway to a world of culinary delights and seamless parcel deliveries. Sign up effortlessly using your mobile number. Your security and privacy are paramount to us, and your number helps ensure a secure and personalized experience. Once registered, you'll be welcomed into a world of possibilities. Explore our extensive list of partner restaurants, eateries, and shops. Discover a diverse array of cuisines, delectable dishes, and essential items available at your fingertips.

place, review and confirm your order

Found what you're craving for? Great! Select your desired items, customize your order as per your preferences, and add them to your basket. Take a moment to review your order, ensuring it's exactly what you desire. Verify the details and quantities. Then, with a simple tap, confirm your choices.

Begin your parcel order by specifying the type, size, and weight of the parcel. You can also indicate the quantity if you have multiple items to send. In the item description, provide any necessary information about the contents of the parcel. For added convenience, you can even upload an image of the parcel. Enter the delivery destination details, including the recipient's phone number. Ensuring accurate information here is crucial to a smooth delivery process. Take a moment to review all the details you've entered for your parcel order. Confirm that everything is accurate, and make any necessary adjustments.

Secure payment

Now, it's time to make your payment. We offer a variety of secure payment options, so you can choose the one that suits you best. Rest assured, your transactions are encrypted and protected. Once your payment is complete, your order is sent to our dedicated team. They swing into action, carefully preparing your food or packaging your parcel with the utmost care. We dispatch our skilled delivery professionals to collect your order swiftly. Our commitment to speed means your delivery is in transit before you know it.

wait for your order

While your order is en route, you can track its progress in real-time using our app. Know exactly when it will arrive at your doorstep. Once the OTP is verified, our team will make sure the parcel is delivered to the recipient. You'll receive a notification once the delivery is complete.
Finally, you receive your order at your doorstep, precisely as you envisioned it. Whether it's a hot, delicious meal or an essential parcel, we're here to bring it to you safely and with a smile. The moment you've been waiting for is here. Whether you're indulging in a sumptuous meal or receiving an important parcel, enjoy every second of it. Your satisfaction is our ultimate reward.
